What I Do

Interior Design

One of my passions is making the most of a space and incorporating as many natural elements as I can while maintaining a sophisticated atmosphere. Reusability is one of the main focuses of my work as I attempt to bring the natural, raw beauty of the outdoors into the comforts of a home. Also, throw pillows!

Photography

From nature to industrial architecture, I enjoy finding the beauty in every space. By capturing these moments in a photograph, I am able to incorporate elements in other creative avenues that may not have immediately presented themselves otherwise. A photograph should speak for itself, much like the design of a space.

Who I Am

Interior Design Student

Although it has been several years since I have attended school of any kind, I have always had a passion for art and design, both in my academic and personal life. In terms of my design style, I have been told I have a whimsical personality; however, I like to create sophisticated designs with a focus on natural elements. In other terms, I want to bring the beauty of nature indoors. With that being said, I personally love the challenge of taking a single design element such as a throw pillow (you can never have too many throw pillows) and designing an entire room around that one element

Once I complete my schooling, I hope to create my own design firm and ultimately turn my hobby into a career with a focus on integrity and reusability. Follow my journey and see my creations!
